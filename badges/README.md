## Usage

Pick the badge matching your conformance level and add it near the top of your `README.md`. Link it to your `.osop-compat.json` or the OSOP spec for context.

Example with link:
```markdown
[![OSOP Validate](https://img.shields.io/badge/OSOP-Validate_v1.0-green)](https://github.com/osop/spec)
```
